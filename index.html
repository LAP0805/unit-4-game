Crystal Game

You are given a random number to match at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below, by clicking a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

Win the game by matching your total score to the random number given.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it

When the game restarts a new random number appears and the values for each crystal changes.
